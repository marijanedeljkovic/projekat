ABOUT:

Mike Miami is a 20 year old DJ/Producer from Serbia.
Mike's music excels at finding a perfect balance between signature emotional melodies, spacy ambience and hard-hitting festival bangers.
This can be seen in his latest releases such as Remedy, Back Around and Ederlezi.
Seeing support from the likes of Hardwell, Blasterjaxx, W&W & many more, Mike continues to push boundaries in the underground festival music scene!
